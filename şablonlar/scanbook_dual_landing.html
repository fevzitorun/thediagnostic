COMMON QUESTIONS

Questions answered.

Do we need CQC registration? +
Yes — all ScanBook partner centres must hold current CQC registration. This protects both patients and your centre's reputation.
What happens if a patient doesn't show? +
Payment is collected upfront. Cancellations within 24 hours receive a full refund. Late cancellations — the centre retains 50% of the booking fee.
Can we control which slots are available? +
Completely. You decide exactly which slots appear on ScanBook. Your existing patients and direct bookings always take priority.
How quickly do we receive payment? +
Standard partners: 14-day net. API partners: weekly payouts. Enterprise: bespoke payment schedule agreed at contract stage.
Can we set our own prices? +
Yes. You control your pricing completely. ScanBook displays your prices transparently — this builds patient trust and increases conversion rates.
