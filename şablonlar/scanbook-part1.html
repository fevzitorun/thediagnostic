FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you need? Our team is happy to help.

Call 0800 123 4567
Do I need a GP referral?+
No. You can self-refer and book directly through ScanBook without any letter, referral, or prior appointment.
How quickly can I get an appointment?+
Many of our partner centres have availability within 24–48 hours. Use the date filter on the search page to find the earliest slot near you.
Who reports my scan?+
All scans are reported by a UK-qualified consultant radiologist. You receive a full written clinical report, not just images.
What is the difference between 1.5T and 3T MRI?+
Tesla (T) refers to magnetic field strength. 3T offers higher resolution, particularly for brain, cardiac and oncological imaging. For most musculoskeletal and routine scans, 1.5T produces excellent results at lower cost.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?+
Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before your appointment. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. See our full cancellation policy in the Terms & Conditions.
Can I use health insurance?+
Yes. We accept Bupa, AXA Health, Aviva, Vitality, WPA, and Cigna at most partner centres. Contact your insurer first to confirm coverage and obtain a pre-authorisation number.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a GP referral for a private MRI?+
No. You can self-refer and book directly through ScanBook without any GP letter or referral.
What is the difference between 1.5T and 3T MRI?+
Tesla (T) refers to magnetic field strength. 3T offers higher resolution, particularly for brain, cardiac and oncological imaging. For most musculoskeletal and routine scans, 1.5T produces excellent results at lower cost.
How long does an MRI take?+
Most single-area scans take 30–45 minutes. A full body MRI takes 2–3 hours. Allow extra time for check-in.
When will I receive my report?+
All partner centres provide a full written radiologist's report within 24–48 hours to your secure portal.
