Security & Compliance

Your data. Fully protected.

🔒
End-to-End Encryption
All patient data, scan results and communications are encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Zero plaintext storage.
🇬🇧
GDPR & UK Data Protection Act
Fully ICO registered. We are a Data Controller and Processor under UK GDPR. Data never leaves UK/EEA servers. Right to erasure supported.
🏥
Verified Clinic Network
Every partner clinic undergoes ID verification, CQC registration check, indemnity insurance validation and quality audit before listing.
🛡️
Multi-Factor Authentication
All clinic and GP accounts require 2FA. Patients can enable biometric login. Session timeouts and device management built-in.
💳
PCI DSS Compliant Payments
Payments processed exclusively via Stripe (Level 1 PCI DSS). We never store card numbers. 3D Secure authentication on all transactions.
📋
Health Questionnaire Screening
MRI safety screening (metallic implants, claustrophobia, pregnancy) is mandatory for all relevant scan types before booking confirms.
