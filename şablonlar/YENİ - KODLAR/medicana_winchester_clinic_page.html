ABOUT THE CENTRE

Medicana Diagnostic Centre
Winchester

Medicana Diagnostic Centre Winchester is a CQC registered private imaging facility offering a full range of diagnostic and screening services in the heart of Hampshire. We provide fast access to expert imaging without the need for a GP referral.

Our centre is equipped with a Siemens 3 Tesla MRI scanner, a low-dose CT system, and dedicated ultrasound suites. All scans are reported by UK Consultant Radiologists with subspecialty expertise.

We also have a warm, private Baby Scan suite where partners and family members are welcome. Our sonographers are BMUS registered and experienced in all stages of pregnancy.
